    üí™ About Mama Said This

    Hey, I'm Heather. Mom of 4. Master's Degree. Self-taught developer at 2am while my kids slept and the house was finally quiet.

    My mom died when I was 19, pregnant with my first. Food banks kept us fed. Emergency C-sections saved my life twice. A pandemic hit when I had newborn twins. Life has been the best teacher I never wanted.

    It's bad enough going through tough times without hitting paywalls on tools that should help. Every "free" resource that wanted $29.99 after the trial made me angrier. So I learned to code. Then I kept building.

    These aren't just tools. They're what I wish existed when I was drowning. When I needed a paystub for an apartment. When I needed to budget on $40. When I needed to homeschool because we couldn't afford anything else.

    For Carrie Hilliard, Donna Chambers, and Elva McConnell ‚Äî who gave without asking why, who helped without judgment, who showed me what real community looks like. This is me paying it forward. üíñ
